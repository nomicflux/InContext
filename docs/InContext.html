    graph(list) View Source (macro)

    Graph construction macro.

    Example

    To create a graph with edges from 1 to 2, 2 to 3, and 1 to 3:

    iex> use InContext
iex> g = graph do
...>   1 ~> 2 ~> 3 <~ 1
...> end

    For a graph with an edge from 1 to 2 and 2 back to 1, and from 4 to 5 and 6 to 5:

    iex> use InContext
iex> g = graph do
...>   1 <~> 2
...>   4 ~> 5 <~ 6
...> end
